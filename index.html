What Is DataRes


DataRes @ UCLA is founded by data enthusiasts who love solving problems and crafting stories with data. The organization attempts to explore various sects of student life and provide data-driven insights to assist student decision-making at UCLA.


We provide a platform for aspiring data scientists and analysts to work on savvy data projects outside of class that are geared towards making an impact. If you love playing with data, creating powerful visualizations, analyzing hidden trends or building predictive models, we would love to have you on the team.


Our Projects

Energy Dashboard


A collaboration with Clean Consulting to show Bruins the analytics behind their daily energy consumption. The goal is to analyze the data collected from the energy meters in Hitch and Saxon and display energy usage on an interactive dashboard using dynamic data visualizations and predictive modelling. This would motivate Bruins on the hill to decrease their energy consumption.

Gendered Pronoun Resolution


Pronoun resolution is the task of pairing a pronoun to what it is referring to in text. This is an important task for natural language understanding, and the resolution of ambiguous pronouns is a longstanding challenge. The goal of the Google AI Gendered Pronoun Resolution Challenge is to create pronoun resolution systems that perform equally well regardless of the pronoun gender.
